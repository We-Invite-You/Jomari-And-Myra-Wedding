Frequently asked questions

When is the RSVP deadline?

Please RSVP before November 30 2024, so we can have an accurate headcount. :)

Am I allowed to bring a plus one?

Please inform us ahead of time so we can have it check and confirm.

What will the weather be like?

The weather here can be funny. During the day, the weather in our venue should be beautiful, averaging 30-35 degrees, but will get chilly and windy at afternoon to night. Please bring some warm layers for the evening, we don't want anyone freezing their bu...well you get the point. ;)

Where should I park?

There is plenty of free parking and the guard will assist you where to park.

What time should I arrive?

Help us get the party started as scheduled! We recommend that you arrive before the start of the ceremony to make sure everyone is seated on time.

What should I wear?

Formal or Semi Formal attire with a touch of color our pallet for both men and women.


What kind of shoes should/shouldn't I wear?

Any kind of shoes you are comfortable but we encourage to wear formal shoes.

Is it okay to take pictures with our phones and cameras during the wedding?

Yes! We would love for you to take photos and share them. However, please refrain from taking photos during the ceremony.

What should I do if I confirmed to the RSVP but didn't able to come?

We truly wished for your presence on our wedding day but if you really can't come, it would be immensely helpful if you could inform us ahead of time.

Whom should I call with questions?

Please reach us on our facebook messenger: Myra Rosales or Jomari Rodriguez
